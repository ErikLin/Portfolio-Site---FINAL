Music App

Following my passion for music I decided to design a music app that would emphasise orginization and an artists visuals. This projet is constantly evolving as I delve deeper into what the perfect music app should look like.

Desktop artist page

Each artist has their own header image and the ability to use a custom font for their name. There are also sections for the usual discography and bio in adition to merch and tickets sections that could help a service like this profit. The music page has sections for popular songs, albums, EP's, singles, videos, and tracks the artist is featured on. The final section is for suggested similar artists, songs, albums, playlists and radio stations.
