Metagalactic Mass

I was given a grid and tasked with creating a font. The result was Metagalactic Mass. The second task was to design a specimine sheet for the new font. I decided that my font gave off a spacey vibe, so I modeled my poster after a galaxy. One of the constraints of the project was that everything on it must come from the type. So the stars are asterisk, commas, and periods, and the shape in the center is two O's.
